Qianqian Wang

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Cornell Tech, Cornell University, advised by Prof. Noah Snavely and Prof. Bharath Hariharan. Before that I received my bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Xiaowei Zhou. I am a recipient of 2022 Google PhD fellowship.

My research lie at the intersection of 3D Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Machine Learning.

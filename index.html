Qianqian Wang

I am a postdoctoral researcher at UC Berkeley, working with Prof. Angjoo Kanazawa and Prof. Alyosha Efros. Additionally, I'm a visiting researcher at Google Research. I completed my PhD in Computer Science at Cornell Tech, Cornell University with my advisors Prof. Noah Snavely and Prof. Bharath Hariharan. Before that I received my bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University, where I worked with Prof. Xiaowei Zhou.
